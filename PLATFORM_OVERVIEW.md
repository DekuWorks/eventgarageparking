**Event Garage Parking** is a web-based peer-to-peer parking marketplace, similar to Airbnb but for parking spaces.

### The Core Concept
Instead of paying $40 to park in a crowded lot far from the venue, event attendees can:
- Book a homeowner's driveway or garage
- Park closer to the event
- Pay less (typically $10-25)
- Walk shorter distances
- Have a guaranteed spot
